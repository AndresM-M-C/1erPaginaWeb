Machu Picchu - Peru

Machu Picchu is an ancient Inca city located in the Peruvian Andes. It is famous for its stunning landscapes and unique architecture.

Machu Picchu was built by the Incas in the 15th century and is believed to have been used as an estate or sacred city for the Inca emperor. It is located on top of a mountain and surrounded by exuberant vegetation.

The city of Machu Picchu has impressive architecture, with temples, agricultural terraces, and carefully built stone houses. The city is divided into several sections, including the agricultural sector, the urban sector, and the sacred sector.

Machu Picchu is considered one of the wonders of the world and has been declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO. Every year, thousands of visitors from all over the world travel to Peru to explore this fascinating Inca city and enjoy the panoramic views of the Andes.
