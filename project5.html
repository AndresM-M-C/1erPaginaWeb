Chichen Itza - Mexico

Chichén Itzá is an ancient Mayan city located on the Yucatán peninsula in Mexico. It is famous for its impressive architecture and historical significance.

Chichén Itzá was built by the Maya in the Late Classic period and became one of the most important centers of Mayan civilization. It is known for its emblematic structures, such as the Kukulcán Pyramid (also known as El Castillo), the Temple of the Warriors and the Observatory.

The archaeological site of Chichén Itzá is considered a World Heritage Site by UNESCO and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World. Every year, thousands of tourists visit this impressive site to explore its unique architecture and learn about the ancient Mayan civilization.
