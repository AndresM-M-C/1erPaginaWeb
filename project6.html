About the Colosseum in Rome

The Colosseum in Rome is an ancient amphitheater located in the center of the city of Rome, Italy. It is one of the most famous monuments in the world and an iconic symbol of ancient Rome. Built in the 1st century AD, the Colosseum was used for various public events, such as gladiatorial battles, theatrical performances, and sports competitions.

The Colosseum is one of Rome's top tourist attractions, attracting millions of visitors each year. Its impressive architecture and rich history make it a fascinating place to explore. In addition, the Colosseum is considered one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

Basic information

  • Location: Colosseum in Rome, Italy
  • Year of construction: 1st century AD
  • Architectural style: Roman architecture
  • Capacity: Approximately 50,000 spectators
